    Grids

    Basic Grids

    You can divide the content in grids. Grids are divided in sixteen columns.

    
<div class="ui grid">
	<div class="two wide column">
		<div class="ui box">two</div>
	</div>
	<div class="three wide column">
		<div class="ui box">three</div>
	</div>
	<div class="four wide column">
		<div class="ui box">four</div>
	</div>
	<div class="seven wide column">
		<div class="ui box">seven</div>
	</div>
</div>
    two
    three
    four
    seven

    Grid in a Grid

    You can put more grids in a grid

    
<div class="ui grid twelve wide">
	<div class="column">
		<div class="ui very compact box">
			<div class="ui grid">
				<div class="ten column wide"><div class="ui very dotted compact red box"></div></div>
				<div class="six column wide"><div class="ui very dotted compact green box"></div></div>
			</div>
		</div>
	</div>
	<div class="column">
		<div class="ui very compact box">
			<div class="ui grid">
				<div class="six column wide"><div class="ui very solid compact blue box"></div></div>
				<div class="ten column wide"><div class="ui very solid compact orange box"></div></div>
			</div>
		</div>
	</div>
</div>

    Responsive Grids

    Grids can take the full width on mobile or tablets if they are responsive.

    
<div class="ui responsive grid">
	<div class="eight wide column">
		<h2 class="ui underline title">Eight</h2>
		<p>Will take full width on smaller screens.</p>
	</div>
	<div class="eight wide column">
		<h2 class="ui underline title">Eight</h2>
		<p>Will take full width on smaller screens.</p>
	</div>
</div>
