Thank you for registering at Avalon; the Legend Lives

Greetings and Welcome!

We do not spam our users with unsolicited marketing e-mails. As a new user, you will be asked in game if you wish to receive helpful scrolls along your journey through novicehood to fully fledged player. It is highly advised for you to accept this help, as we will e-mail you specific help files and documents that will make your journey much easier to follow. However, you will always be in control of the e-mails you receive from us!

For help and support with any aspect of Avalon, please don't hesitate to e-mail us at Avalon Support. You'll find us friendly and willing to help, no matter what the issue may be.

What's Next?

Much more information can be found at our web site at avalon-rpg.com

There is an introductory manual available for reading at our Intro section, which can be found at Avalon Introduction. Dependant upon your experience as a roleplayer, some or all of it will help you familiarise yourself with the Avalon realm. Your next steps in the realm, should be to follow your guides and friendly adventurers. They will introduce you to the basics of the land and eventually help you integrate into a guild and profession, where you will gain skills and abilities that will increase your character's strength and potential influence. All this information can also be read about in the Introductory Guide mentioned above. However, until you have joined a guild your best course of action is to follow your adventurers' guidance, referring to the manuals and website if needed.

It is important to remember that Avalon is not a quick "hack 'n' slash" type game; we think of it more as a living, breathing ever evolving world created by the imaginations of the mortals and gods who exist within it. It is a roleplaying game that will give as much as you put in to it. If you are patient, imaginative and willing to learn its intracacies, then there is quite literarlly an entire world for you to explore and become a living part of.

If all else fails and you are quite simply not sure what to do type OON and include your question or message e.g OON I NEED HELP. There will nearly always be someone on your continent who will be willing to help.

Problems Connecting?

Or perhaps you found your screen to be confusing while playing? The web site also includes browser based software for connecting to Avalon - click on the 'Play Now' button at the top right of the page. Once you have filled in the form found there, an in browser experience will load, which is the fastest and easiest way to play Avalon.

Simply fill in the form on the Play Now page and choose at the bottom of the form whether or not you would like to use Flash or Java. For Flash click on the Lumiere option; if you prefer Java then click on the Javalon option. If you're not sure, then it is best to leave the option as Lumiere, as this will work with virtually all browsers. Note: only the Javalon option will work with iPads and iPhones.

Advanced Roleplayer?

We also have a list of recommended downloadable clients you can use to connect to Avalon, which is updated regularly. These clients tend to be for the more advanced Mud player as they require a modicum of setting up. Simply visit our Mud Clients page for more information. For this software, you will need to connect to avalon with the following details:

  • Host: avalon-rpg.com
  • Port: 23
